Mountain Lake Putt-Putt Golf Center

Thank you for visitng the website for Mountain Lake Putt-Putt Golf Center. We are located in the North Georgia Mountains in the city of Helen, Georgia. Last year, many tourists who visited our city enjoyed our award winning putt-putt golf course.

The Atlanta Journal listed Mountain Lake Putt-Putt Golf Center as one of the top five putt-putt golf courses in the state last year. Our golf course even hosted the National Putt-Putt Championships in 2002.

The next time you visit Helen, Georgia, stop by and play a game of putt-putt golf. If you mention this website, you will receive a 10% discount during the month of September. We look forward to seeing you soon!

Some special features of our golf course include:

  • Site of the 2002 National Putt-Putt Championship
  • Awarded the Most Challenging Putt-Putt Golf Course in 2008
  • Excellent restaurant and video arcade located next to the golf course
  • Lessons taught by the 1997 National Putt-Putt Champion, Bill Jones

Directions from Atlanta:

  1. GA 400 North. Take 400 North until it ends.
  2. Continue ahead on GA Hwy. 115
  3. Go 5 miles to the traffic light.
  4. Turn right onto Hwy. 115.
  5. Go 13 miles to Cleveland.
  6. At traffics light, turn left onto US 129 North.
  7. Go to the next traffic light and turn right on Hwy. 75 North.
  8. 9 miles to Helen.
